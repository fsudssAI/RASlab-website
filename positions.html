Prospective Graduate Students

We are always looking for bright and motivated students with CS/CE/EE/ECE backgrounds. Candidates are expected to have solid background/interest in one of the following areas:

• Computer Security and Embedded Systems

• Power Grid Cyber-Physical Systems

• Control Theory and Optimization

• Machine Learning and Deep Neural Networks

Required Skills/Criteria:

• Strongly motivated

• Excellent programming skills: C/C++, Python, Matlab

If you would like to work under my supervision, please contact me at ckonstantinouieee.org if interested. Attach your transcripts, resume, and any publications (in ONE PDF). In addition, send me the a short summary that:

1) Shows you have already looked at my previous work.

2) Tells me the research direction(s) you are interested in.

3) Mentions any relevant background you might have for these directions.

I will usually have a Skype call with promising candidates. To show me you have actually taken the time to read this page, include the word “cyber” at the beginning of your email. Finally, I highly recommend to read first Prof. Wilson's guide for joining my lab.

Please accept my apology that due to the large number of emails I receive, only shortlisted candidates will be replied.
