DSS lab supports research and education in modeling and simulation of large-scale systems, cybersecurity, industrial control, smart grid security, and embedded systems.

Latest News

Feb 10, 2021

Our paper “Cyber-Physical Energy Systems Security: Threat Modeling, Risk Assessment, Resources, Metrics, and Case Studies” has been accepted to IEEE Access. Congratulations to Yannis, Juan, and Shelly. You may find the paper here.

Jan 29, 2021

Our vision paper “Towards a Secure and Resilient All-Renewable Energy Grid for Smart Cities” has been accepted to IEEE Consumer Electronics Magazine. You may find the paper here.

Jan 14, 2021

A review collaborative paper led by Dr. Mazumder, “A Review of Current Research Trends in Power-Electronic Innovations in Cyber-Physical Systems” has been accepted to IEEE Journal of Emerging and Selected Topics in Power Electronics. You may find the paper here.

Jan 7, 2021

Dr. Konstantinou has been nominated and selected as an ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) Distinguished Speaker for a 3-year term: Link.

Dec 26, 2020

Our paper “On the Feasibility of Load-Changing Attacks in Power Systems during the COVID-19 Pandemic” has been accepted to IEEE Access. Congratulations to Juan and Shelly. You may find the paper here.

“ConFirm: Detecting Firmware Modifications in Embedded Systems using Hardware Performance Counters”, presented in ICCAD 2015, has been selected as a Top Pick in Hardware and Embedded Security ’20 (most influential papers for 2012-2017)! Link to paper, Presentation.
